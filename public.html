How It Works

  • The college issues certificates on the Ethereum Sepolia blockchain with a unique ID.
  • Each certificate's metadata is stored on IPFS (decentralized storage).
  • Anyone can verify by entering the Certificate ID — the blockchain returns its status instantly.
  • Revoked certificates are flagged and cannot be falsely verified.
